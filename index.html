Crystal Collector!!

How this works:

You will be given a number at the start of the game.

There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your score below.

You win the Game by matching the total score to the random number, you lose if your total score goes over the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the values of the crystals will be changed.
